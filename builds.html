August 2019 Build

This build was completed for an acquaintance transitioning from Xbox who had no idea where to start with a PC. Based on the games he wanted to play (Destiny 2 and CS:GO) and his budget, we were able to put together a parts list, order his parts, and put together his PC in just a week! This system is capable of playing most games at 1440p at 100+ Hz, and cost around $1100, excluding peripherals like a monitor, mouse, and keyboard.

July 2019 Build

This is one of more expensive builds. Our client requested the highest possible spec to play the newest games at the highest resolution and framerate possible. Due to some of these parts being out of stock, this took a little longer to build, almost 2 weeks, but we were able to use our connections to get the hard to find parts into the build. This build cost around $2700, and is capable of playing most games at 4K/60+Hz, or 1440p/240+Hz.
